About

My Story

Originally from Berlin, I have lived in international contexts for almost 10 years now. I've practiced and loved yoga since 2010 when I discovered it at a vipasana retreat in Thailand. After years of practice I felt ready to pass on my knowledge to others. I've thus completed a 200h Yoga Alliance certified teacher training in Rishikesh, India at the start of 2018. At the beautiful Association for Yoga and Meditation I was trained in Hatha and Ashtanga Vinyasa. I am thus familiar with some of the more traditional roots of yoga. In my teachings, I combine these traditional elements (including philosophy and anatomy) with some more modern aspects of  Vinyasa yoga. I am as passionate about yoga as I am about sustainability. Having completed my M.A. in Sustainable Design at the University of Brighton, I strive to combine my yoga practice with my passion for all things sustainable. I hope to meet you soon in class!
